What We Address

Concrete Holds On to
Everything It Touches

Porous by nature, concrete absorbs oil, fertiliser, leaf tannin, and the algae that thrives in coastal BC shade. Surface cleaning equipment, the right cleaning solution, and a steady hand return the surface to its original tone.

Surface cleaner attachment for even, line-free results
