Why It Matters

Pressure Washing
Belongs Off the Roof

Pressure on shingles strips granules, voids warranties, and shortens roof life. The right approach is a soft-wash treatment that kills the organic growth at the source. The roof keeps looking better, longer, with no surface damage.

Safe for asphalt shingles, metal roofs, and tile
Targets gloeocapsa magma, lichen, moss, and algae
Granules stay put, manufacturer warranties stay valid
Includes valley clearing and gutter exterior brightening

Our Approach

A Roof Wash
Done Properly

01
Inspect
Identify roofing material, problem zones, vulnerable flashings, and skylight seals.
02
Protect
Saturate landscaping, cover delicate plants, secure adjacent surfaces and downspout outlets.
03
Treat
Apply soft-wash solution at low pressure. Allow dwell time so growth dies at the root.
04
Rinse & Verify
Light rinse where appropriate, walk results with you, document anything to monitor next visit.
